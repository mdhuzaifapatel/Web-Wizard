Rules And Instructions

Rules
Aim: Solve the questions, to get the files required to develop the website.

  1. The competition is of 3 hours in total and will consist of 2 parts.
  2. The first part consists of solving a set of puzzles to get the access of required website files.
  3. These puzzles require you to think outside the box.
  4. Each puzzle will give you a set of letters, enter them into a form which can be accessed by clicking the "Enter your answers here" buttton
  5. Solve all the questions within 30 mins and you will be able to access the second part of the round early.
  6. Everyone will be provided the website files for the second part of the round after 30 mins.
  7. The second part consists of developing a website.
  8. The team which develops the website first with most significant match is ranked number 1 on the leader board and the next team(s) receive the immediately following ranking number.
  9. Internet access is allowed only for 1st round (Puzzle round).
  10. Internet access is not allowed for 2nd round (Website development).
  11. Only the participants physically present will be considered for the second round.

Declare variables not war, execute programs not people.
