Rules And Instructions

Rules
Aim: Complete the link to get to the to get to the second part

  1. The Round is of 40 minutes in total and will consist of 2 parts.
  2. The first part consists of solving a set of puzzles to complete the required link.
  3. These puzzles require you to think outside the box.
  4. Each puzzle will give you a part of the link complete it within 20 min and you will be able to access the second part of the round early.
  5. Everyone will be provided the link to the second part of the round after 20 min
  6. The second part consists of 2 questions on Hacker Rank.
  7. A program will be considered wrong if it fails to work on one or more of the test cases.
  8. A correct program will be awarded the full points, and an incorrect one zero.
  9. The participant with the highest score is ranked number 1 on the leader board and the next participant(s) receive the immediately following ranking number.
  10. The tiebreaker is the sum of the time of the first correct submission for all the problems.
  11. Internet access is allowed for this round.
  12. The top 12 participants will move on to the next round.
  13. Only the participants physically present will be considered for the second round.
